# How-to-Clean-Bosch-Washing-Machine
How to clean a Bosch front loader washing machine?
* Start by cleaning the detergent drawer with warm water and mild soap.
* Next, run a hot cycle using white vinegar or a washing machine cleaner to remove any leftover residue in the drum.
* Wipe the door seal with a mixture of water and vinegar to get rid of mold and dirt.
* Clean the filter at the bottom front of the machine to remove lint and debris.
* Use a damp cloth with mild detergent to wipe down the outside of the machine.
* Remember to keep the door open between uses to let it air dry, and use the correct amount of detergent.
* Clean the compartments where you put detergent, bleach, and fabric softener when necessary.
* Finally, run a rinse cycle with vinegar to ensure there's no residue left.
